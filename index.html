The problem

Most BOM risk is discovered too late.

Manufacturers often find component issues after a quote is late, a build is delayed, inventory is stranded, or engineering is forced into a reactive redesign. Continuum Components brings structure, visibility, and disciplined analysis to the component lifecycle before those issues disrupt the business.

Services

Lifecycle support for complex component decisions.

BOM Scrubbing

Clean, normalize, and review bills of material to expose missing data, inconsistent part numbers, sourcing concerns, and lifecycle gaps.

Obsolescence Risk Assessment

Identify end-of-life exposure, single-source risk, aging components, and supply continuity concerns before they become urgent.

Alternate Component Identification

Support engineering and procurement teams with structured alternate searches, fit-for-purpose options, and supplier availability review.

Component Lifecycle Management

Create repeatable processes for lifecycle review, change control, documentation, and cross-functional decision making.

Supply Chain Intelligence

Translate component-level data into practical actions for purchasing, inventory, quoting, and production planning.

S&OP and Materials Support

Connect component risk to the broader operating rhythm: demand planning, supply planning, inventory strategy, and execution.
